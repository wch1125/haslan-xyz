Welcome

12 December 2025

Abstract: A personal wiki for aggregating various thoughts and musings on a variety of subjects and projects.

This is a personal wiki—a space for thinking, observing, and connecting ideas across disciplines. Unlike a traditional blog with chronological posts, these pages are evergreen and evolve over time as understanding deepens. This format is meant to better reflect how I experience my own non-linear thought processes about topics. Nothing is immutable, all ideas are continuously refined through continued reflection and re-evaluation.
