Writing

Abstract: Essays, sketches, and creative work. Some polished, some exploratory. The badges tell you what kind of thing you're getting into.

Reading Guide

Each piece has a badge indicating its status:

  • Essay — Argued and structured. I'm making a case.
  • Sketch — Speculative. Exploring, not concluding.
  • Notebook — Working notes. Rough but potentially useful.
  • Fiction — Creative work. Not claims about reality.
  • Draft — Unfinished. Here for accountability.
