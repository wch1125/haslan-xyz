The Symphony Framework

A central metaphor for consciousness: score (causal patterns), instruments (material substrate), conductor (witness-self), music (experience), and audience (Brahman).

  • The Symphony of Consciousness Sketch Dec 2025

    A metaphor for exploring consciousness: score as causal pattern, instruments as material substrate, conductor as witness-self, and music as experience itself.

  • Consciousness as Symphony Sketch Dec 2025

    Exploring consciousness as emergent harmony rather than singular entity, existing across dimensions of integration.

  • The Conductor and the Score Sketch Dec 2025

    The conductor as metaphor for the witness-self—shaping experience without producing it, finding agency even under determinism.

  • The Audience in the Dark Sketch Dec 2025

    Introducing the audience as Brahman—the singular awareness that watches all symphonies, persists across performances.

  • The Ground Beneath the Score Sketch Dec 2025

    What is the relationship between the elements of the symphony metaphor at the level of being? Does the score exist independently?

  • Archetypes as Evolved Schools Sketch Dec 2025

    Archetypal patterns as evolving schools in which conductors train, strengthened or weakened by collective participation.
