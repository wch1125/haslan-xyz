Contact

This site is written under the pseudonym H. Aslan. I read everything that comes in, even if I can't always reply quickly.

Say Hello

For correspondence: haslan [at] proton [dot] me

I typically respond within a few days when time permits. No pressure to be formal—a short note is fine.

What I'd Love to Hear About

  • Thoughts or pushback on anything I've written
  • Corrections, broken links, or suggestions
  • Your own personal site (seriously—send me the link!)
  • Collaboration ideas
  • Just saying hello

Show Me Your Site

If you have a personal website, blog, wiki, or digital garden—I want to see it. Seriously. It doesn't have to be polished or "done." The whole point of personal domains is that they're living things.

Email me a link and I'll actually look at it. I might even link to it from here if you're open to that.

I believe the web gets better when more people have their own corners of it. If you're thinking about making one but don't know where to start, I'm happy to point you toward resources or just talk through options.

Probably Not a Good Fit

  • Unsolicited pitches or guest post requests
  • Anything requiring me to sign an NDA before hearing the idea
  • Requests to "pick my brain" that are actually consulting

(No hard feelings—just being upfront.)
